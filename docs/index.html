ENGLISH

Support for Instructors

  • Create a multiple-choice tests, with up to 12 questions. Each answer must have 4 answer alternatives, labelled A, B, C, D.
  • Distribute this test to your students in whichever form you find suitable, digitally or on paper.
  • Note down the sequence of right answers.
  • Open the Nøtteknekker, and click on the + button on the start screen.
  • Enter your solution sequence.
  • Distribute the generated code to your students.

ENGLISH

Support for Students

  • Open the Nøtteknekker, and enter the code provided by your instructor.
  • Click Start
  • Select a question to solve.
  • For the question, select what you consider is the best answer and click it.
